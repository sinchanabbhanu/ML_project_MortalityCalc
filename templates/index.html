Covid-19 Mortality Risk Prediction

Intent: Our project is to use machine learning to identify patient populations at risk for COVID-19 mortality.

Data used for the project was based on a compilation of international clinical publications created by the team at COVID Analytics. Each row represents a cohort of patients with a rich amount of medical information: such as percentage breakdown of gender, ethnicity, past travel and medical history, symptoms, treatment plan and mean population values for age, lab results (CBC, WBC, liver enzymes) to list a few.

We focused on age, gender, medical history (pre-existing conditions) and symptoms. The dataset was filtered to include populations with adequately reported data and the dataset size was augmented to allow for application of machine learning. All filtered population data for modeling were tested COVID-19 positive. To apply logistic regression model in determining populations at low or high COVID-19 mortality risk, the rate of mortality was converted to binary values using the mean as a sorting factor.
